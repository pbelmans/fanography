---
title: List of Fano varieties for which Bott vanishing holds
layout: bott
---

Bott vanishing holds for a smooth projective variety $X$ if $\mathrm{H}^j(X,\Omega_X^i\otimes\mathcal{L})=0$ for all $j\geq 1$, $i\geq 0$ and $\mathcal{L}\in\operatorname{Pic}(X)$ *ample*.

The Fano 3-folds for which Bott vanishing holds (resp. fails) were classified by Totaro in [Bott vanishing for Fano 3-folds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.08142v1). Earlier he showed that del Pezzo surfaces of degree at least 5 satisfy Bott vanishing.
